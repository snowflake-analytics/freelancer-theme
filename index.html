About

Snowflake Analytics specialises in support, maintenance and development of business systems that build on and extend the Snowplow analytics ecosystem. It was formed by three technologists with broad real-world experience in Snowplow, data analytics and event databases.

Our founding team of experts has deep, broad and hands-on experience in digital analytics, cross-channel marketing, ad tech, data science, IPTV tracking, media measurement and digital optimization.
